**Get the list of all associations defined on a Model**

```ruby
Doctor.reflections.collect{|a, b| {b.macro => b.class_name}}.compact

#=>
[
	  {:has_many=>"Device"}, 
	  {:has_many=>"Call"}, 
	  {:has_many=>"Feedback"}, 
	  {:has_one=>"Document"}
]
```

**List of all callbacks**

```ruby
ActiveRecord::Callbacks::CALLBACKS
```
